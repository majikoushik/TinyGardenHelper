# Privacy and Child Safety Notes

> [!WARNING]
> **DRAFT GUIDANCE**: The final forms submitted to Google Play and Apple App Store must be completed based on the *actual* final build contents. The text below assumes the app follows the strict offline, ad-free constraints of this codebase.

This document contains text templates to be used when filling out the Apple App Store Privacy Nutrition Labels and the Google Play Data Safety form.

## Apple App Store: Privacy Nutrition Labels
When configuring the App Store Connect record:
- **Data Collection**: No
- **Does your app collect data from this app?**: No.
- **Kids Category**: Check the box to enroll the app in the Kids Category (Ages 5 and Under, or Ages 6-8).

## Google Play: Data Safety Form
When configuring the Google Play Console:
- **Data Collection and Security**: Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types? **No**.
- **Families Program**: Opt-in to the Families program. Target ages: **3-5** and **6-8**.
- **Ads**: Does your app contain ads? **No**.
